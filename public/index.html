Semantic Feature Net

Welcome to SeFNet: Semantic Feature Net, a website providing a comprehensive collection of medical datasets used in machine learning tasks. Each dataset has an associated set of term annotations from SNOMED-CT ontology. Most of the datasets are provided in .csv with their basic characteristics and sources.
